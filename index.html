Who We Are

Positioning Abuja as a Distinct Cultural Identity

Founded in 2010 as a direct offshoot of the Ford Foundation mentorship programme, IICD has spent over a decade working to develop the Abuja art scene as a distinctive cultural force — dismantling the stereotype of the city as a soulless metropolis.

We operate as an open thematic experimental art space, infusing pedagogical and creative processes into the industry. From residencies and exhibitions to open houses and capacity building, everything we do serves one goal: helping artists establish their voice, connect their paths, and create new narratives.
