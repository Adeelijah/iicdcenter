International Artist Residency — Open Call

Residency Programme · IICD Centre, Maitama, Abuja

Applications for the next IICD residency cycle will open soon. The 30-day programme is open to Nigerian and international artists, with a focus on contemporary practice, cultural exchange, and sustainability. Fees from USD 1,000 (Abuja-based Nigerian artists) to USD 2,500 (international). See the Residency page for full details.

Curatorial Classes — New Cohort

Training Programme · IICD Centre, Maitama

IICD's structured curatorial training programme will open for a new cohort. The programme offers rigorous learning, direct mentorship from Nduwhite Ndubuisi Ahanonu, and hands-on curatorial practice through IICD's live exhibition programme. Open to aspiring curators and cultural managers at all career stages.
